# BeyondLabel — Competitive Analysis
Version: 1.0
Author: Mrigank Raj Chouhan
Date: June 2026

## Market Overview

The food label analysis space has existing players but none 
solve the specific problem of AI-powered, goal-personalized, 
plain-English verdict generation for Indian packaged food 
products with India-specific manipulation detection.

## Competitor Breakdown

### Yuka
- Market: Global, Europe-first
- Input method: Barcode scan
- Analysis type: Static database score (0-100)
- Explains WHY: No
- India-specific: No
- Goal personalization: No
- Key weakness: Not built for Indian market, no reasoning 
  layer, just a score

### FactsScan
- Market: India
- Input method: Barcode scan
- Analysis type: Static database score
- Explains WHY: No
- India-specific: Partial — follows FSSAI guidelines
- Goal personalization: Dietary preference only
- Key weakness: Database-dependent, cannot catch nuanced 
  manipulation tactics, no AI reasoning

### TruthLabel (truthlabel.ai)
- Market: Global
- Input method: Text and photo
- Analysis type: Basic AI
- Explains WHY: No
- India-specific: No
- Goal personalization: No
- Key weakness: Vibe-coded weekend project, no Indian market 
  focus, no goal personalization

## BeyondLabel Differentiation

| Feature | Yuka | FactsScan | TruthLabel | BeyondLabel |

## Our Unfair Advantage

Every competitor returns a score. We return a reason.
A score tells you what to think. A reason teaches you how 
to think. That is the difference between a tool users open 
once and a tool users trust.

## Gaps We Are Exploiting

1. No competitor explains the specific manipulation tactic 
   being used in plain English
2. No competitor personalizes analysis to a specific health 
   goal like muscle gain or diabetes management
3. No competitor is built specifically around Indian label 
   manipulation tricks — soy inflation, jaggery as no sugar, 
   serving size deception
4. All competitors require a barcode — we work with just 
   a product name or photo

## What To Watch

FactsScan is the closest Indian competitor. Monitor if they 
add AI reasoning or goal personalization — that would reduce 
our differentiation. Yuka expanding to India aggressively 
would also be a threat worth tracking.
