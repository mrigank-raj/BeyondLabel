PRODUCT NAME: BeyondLabel

PROBLEM STATEMENT:
Health-conscious Indian consumers are being misled by 
front-of-pack claims on packaged food products. Labels say 
"High Protein," "Zero Sugar," and "Natural" — but FSSAI 
regulations remain too weak to enforce honest labeling, and 
most consumers lack the nutritional expertise to decode 
ingredient lists themselves. Existing tools like Yuka and 
FactsScan return a numerical score but never explain why a 
product is misleading, don't personalize to the user's health 
goal, and miss India-specific manipulation tactics like soy 
protein inflation, jaggery marketed as "no refined sugar," or 
serving size deception. BeyondLabel solves this by using AI to 
analyze any Indian packaged food product and return a 
plain-English, goal-specific verdict — not a score, but a reason.

WHO HAS THIS PROBLEM:
Health-conscious Indian consumers aged 18–35 who are actively 
trying to make better food choices — gym-goers, fitness 
beginners, diabetics, and parents — who shop on Amazon, 
Blinkit, Flipkart, or in physical stores like DMart. They are 
motivated but misled. They want to eat right but cannot trust 
what brands put on the front of the pack.

WHAT THEY DO TODAY:
Watch YouTube reviews (often sponsored), Google the product, 
use AI manually by copying ingredient lists, or simply guess. 
All of these are slow, unreliable, or require nutritional 
expertise they don't have.

WHAT BEYONDLABEL DOES:
User types a product name or uploads a photo of a food label, 
selects their health goal (Muscle Gain / Fat Loss / General 
Health / Diabetes Management), and receives an instant 
AI-powered verdict: Trustworthy / Question It / Avoid — with 
a plain-English explanation of why, and a specific better 
alternative.

WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT:
- AI reasoning, not a database score
- Explains WHY a product is misleading, not just that it is
- Personalized to the user's specific health goal
- Built specifically for Indian food products and Indian label 
  manipulation tactics
- No barcode scanner needed — works with product name or 
  label photo

CORE DESIGN PRINCIPLE:
Our job is not to help users find healthy products. Our job 
is to help users reject dishonest ones.

WHAT WE ARE NOT BUILDING:
- Restaurant or homemade food analyzer
- Calorie tracker or diet planner
- Barcode scanner with product database
- Social features or community ratings
- User accounts or saved history (V1)

TECH STACK:
- Frontend: React + Tailwind CSS
- AI for text: Groq API
- AI for images: Google Gemini API (vision)
- Hosting: Vercel
- No backend required for MVP

SUCCESS FOR MVP:
A user types "Pintola Protein Oats", selects "Muscle Gain", 
clicks Analyze, and receives a verdict that correctly 
identifies the soy vs whey deception, explains why it matters 
for muscle gain, and suggests a better alternative.
